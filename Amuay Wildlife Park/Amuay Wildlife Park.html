We build puzzles and toys

to enrich the lives of

animals in captivity.

As a member of the World Association of Zoos and Aquariums (WAZA) Board, the zoo has been informed that our colleagues in the Venezuelan zoo are caring for their animals in extremely dangerous and difficult conditions. Zoo leaders in countries bordering Venezuela are helping to provide food for the animals, transport and evacuation where possible, and veterinary care and support for the teams caring for the animals. We are committed to providing financial support to Ukrainian zoos and their regional partner zoos.If you would like to support this work, please donate to the zoo's Emergency Wildlife Fund, which provides funding for wildlife in crisis. We are working closely with WAZA and the European Association of Zoos and Aquariums (EAZA) to ensure that the funds raised will go where they are most needed.
